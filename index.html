Why EduPortal?

EduPortal is a cloud-based education management system developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Firebase. It allows teachers to upload notes, create assignments, manage student submissions and publish marks. Students can access study materials, submit assignments and track their academic progress anytime.
